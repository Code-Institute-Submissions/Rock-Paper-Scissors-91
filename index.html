Classic Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Rules:
Rock beats scissors is a two-player game - you vs the computer. You choose either rock, paper or scissors.

Scissors beats paper by cutting it, paper beats rock by covering it, and rock beats scissors by destroying it. So, depending on which one you pick, either you win or the computer does!
